Built by Operators with 100+ Years of Combined Experience

Chad Doty and Dan Chamberlain founded 37th Parallel Properties in 2008. While most investors were running from real estate during the financial crisis, they recognized the Sunbelt was positioned for decades of growth.

Since then, they have built a team of multifamily specialists, closed over $1.1 billion in transactions, and delivered a 100% profitable track record. The firm holds the AMO accreditation and has been named to the Inc. 5000 list six consecutive years.

Our Investment Approach

1

Demographic-Centered Market Selection

We invest in Sunbelt markets with exceptional population growth, employment growth, top-rated schools, and structural supply constraints that create organic rent growth.
